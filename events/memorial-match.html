Fixtures & Schedule

The Oraifite CYON League commemorates the 15th memorial anniversary of Late Fr. Chinonso Anaedo, organized for both football (male category) and volleyball (female category). Click here for the league table.

General Rules for the Memorial Match of Fr. Stanley Anaedo

1. Match Duration:

Each match will follow the standard duration for football and volleyball unless otherwise specified. Football: 90 minutes (two halves). Volleyball matches: best-of-three or five sets.

2. Punctuality:

All teams must arrive at the venue at least 30 minutes before the scheduled time. Matches will start promptly. Teams arriving late may forfeit the match. After 30 minutes, 3 points will be awarded to the ready team.

3. Team Composition:

Teams must register their players before the tournament. Try to use parishioners instead of external players to honor the memorial intentions.

4. Venue:

Matches will be held at St. Maryâ€™s Parish Field Oraifite. Teams can choose a different venue if agreed upon and inform the coordinator 24 hours in advance.

5. Code of Conduct:

Players, officials, and spectators must exhibit sportsmanship. Violence, abuse, or cheating will lead to disqualification. Proper footwear is required for both football and volleyball.

Specific Rules for Football (Male Category):

1. Group Stage:

Teams are divided into groups (A and B). Points: Win = 3, Draw = 1, Loss = 0.

2. Semi-Finals:

The top two teams from each group will qualify for the semi-finals, with matches played between the first-place and second-place teams.

3. Finals:

Winners of the semi-finals play in the final. Losing semi-finalists contest third place.

4. Tiebreakers:

If scores are tied, penalty shootouts will determine the winner.

Specific Rules for Volleyball (Female Category):

1. Group Stage:

Teams will compete in a round-robin format.

2. Semi-Finals:

The top two teams advance to knockout semi-finals.

3. Finals:

Winners of the semi-finals play in the final. Third-place teams contest second runner-up.

4. Scoring System:

Matches follow standard scoring. A team must win by two points in the deciding set.

5. Substitution:

Each team is allowed up to six substitutions per set.

Additional Notes:

Referees' decisions are final. Teams must carry basic medical supplies for injuries. Unsportsmanlike conduct will attract yellow/red cards or warnings.
